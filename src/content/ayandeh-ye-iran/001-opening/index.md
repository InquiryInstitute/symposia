# Session 001: Opening

**Symposion-e Āyandeh-ye Irān**  
*سمپوزیون آینده‌ی ایران*

## Central Question

> چه آینده‌ای برای ایران متصور است؟  
> **What future can be imagined for Iran? And what role does our intellectual heritage play in shaping it?**

## Session Flow

1. **Ferdowsi** opens with the weight of the Shahnameh, arguing that language carries the soul of a civilization
2. **Saʿdi** responds with practical wisdom - the fire must be fed by daily conduct, not legend alone
3. **Rumi** elevates to the spiritual - love (ishq) as the force that dissolves boundaries
4. **Avicenna** bridges reason and faith - systematic inquiry serves the divine order
5. **Al-Biruni** grounds in the empirical - measurement and observation over speculation
6. **Omar Khayyam** introduces doubt - mathematics is certain, but does certainty fill the cup?
7. **Hafez** interrupts as the heretic - what of beauty, joy, the present moment?
